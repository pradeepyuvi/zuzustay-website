1. Introduction

Welcome to ZUZUStay ("App", "Service", "we", "our", or "us").

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the ZUZUStay mobile application and related services.

By accessing or using ZUZUStay, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We may collect the following information:

  • Name
  • Mobile number
  • Profile photograph (if uploaded)
  • Property details
  • Property address information
  • Property photographs (if uploaded)
  • Tenant information including:
    • Name
    • Mobile number
    • Email address (if provided)
    • Address
    • Room assignment details
    • Tenant photograph (if uploaded)
  • Rental and payment-related information entered by the user
  • Account authentication information
  • Tenant management information may include:
    • Occupancy records
    • Room assignments
    • Check-in and check-out records
    • Blocked tenant records
    • Block reasons entered by authorized users
    • Tenant contact information and communication details entered by authorized property managers

Property owners and managers are solely responsible for obtaining all required permissions, notices, and consents from tenants before uploading tenant personal information, photographs, addresses, contact details, documents, or related records to ZUZUStay.

Users are responsible for ensuring that information entered into tenant block records is lawful, accurate, and necessary for legitimate property management purposes.

2.2 Authentication Information

We use mobile number verification through One-Time Passwords (OTP) to authenticate users and secure access to accounts.

2.3 Usage Information

We may automatically collect:

  • Device model
  • Device operating system
  • Application version
  • App usage statistics
  • Crash reports
  • Diagnostic information
  • Performance metrics

2.4 Device Permissions

Depending on the device and operating system version, ZUZUStay may request access to:

  • Notifications – to provide reminders, important updates, payment-related alerts, and service notifications.
  • Camera – to capture profile photographs, property photographs, and tenant photographs where applicable.
  • Photos / Media Library – to allow users to select and upload images from their device.

Certain Android devices may use the system Photo Picker, which allows users to select images without granting full photo library access.

Permissions are requested only when required for specific features and may be managed through device settings at any time.

8. Account and Data Deletion

Users may submit an account deletion request directly through the Profile section of the application using the available Delete Account feature.

Upon submission, the deletion request may be reviewed for fraud prevention, unauthorized account removal, platform security, misuse prevention, or compliance purposes.

The deletion request enters a processing period of up to 30 days.

During this period:

  • Users may continue to access their account where permitted.
  • Users may withdraw the deletion request by logging in and selecting the available account retrieval option.

Account deletion will not be completed if the request is withdrawn before processing is finalized.

If the deletion request is not withdrawn, the account and associated data will be deleted within 30 days from the original request date, except where retention is required by applicable laws, regulatory obligations, dispute resolution requirements, fraud prevention, security purposes, or legitimate business needs.

Deletion may include removal of:

  • Account information
  • Properties
  • Rooms
  • Tenant records
  • Payment tracking information
  • Occupancy records
  • Check-in and check-out records
  • Blocklist records
  • Uploaded photographs
  • Related account data

Users may also contact us at admin@zuzustay.com for assistance regarding account deletion requests.

9. Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations.

After account deletion, data will generally be removed within 30 days, except where retention is required by:

  • Applicable laws
  • Regulatory requirements
  • Security obligations
  • Fraud prevention requirements
  • Dispute resolution requirements
  • Legitimate business purposes

10. Children's Privacy

ZUZUStay is intended solely for property owners, managers, operators, and authorized staff involved in property management activities.

The application is intended for users who are 18 years of age or older.

We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13 years of age. If we become aware that such information has been collected without appropriate authorization, we will take reasonable steps to delete it.
