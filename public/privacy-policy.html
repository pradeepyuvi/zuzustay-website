Welcome to ZuzuStay ("App", "we", "our", or "us").

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We may collect the following information:

  • Name

    • Name
    • Mobile number

Property owners are responsible for obtaining any necessary consent from tenants before uploading tenant information, photographs, or related records to ZuzuStay.

We use mobile number verification through One-Time Passwords (OTP) to authenticate users and secure access to accounts.

2.3 Usage Information

We may automatically collect:

You may delete your account directly from the application if such functionality is available.

Alternatively, you may submit a deletion request by contacting us at admin@zuzustay.com.

Upon receiving a valid request, we will process account and data deletion within 30 days, unless retention is required by law.

9. Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations.

After account deletion, personal information will generally be removed within 30 days, except where retention is required by applicable laws, regulatory obligations, or legitimate business purposes.

10. Children's Privacy

There is no minimum age requirement to use ZuzuStay. However, users under the age of 18 should use the application under the supervision of a parent, guardian, property owner, or authorized manager.

We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under the age of 13. If we become aware that such information has been collected without appropriate authorization, we will take reasonable steps to delete it.
