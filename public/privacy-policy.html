Welcome to ZUZUStay ("App", "Service", "we", "our", or "us").

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We may collect the following information:

  • Name
  • Mobile number
  • Profile photograph (if uploaded)
  • Property details
  • Property address information
  • Property photographs (if uploaded)
  • Tenant information including:
    • Name
    • Mobile number
    • Email address (if provided)
    • Address
    • Room assignment details
    • Tenant photograph (if uploaded)
  • Rental and payment-related information entered by the user
  • Account authentication information
  • Tenant management information may include:
    • Occupancy records
    • Room assignments
    • Check-in and check-out records

Property owners and managers are responsible for obtaining any necessary consent from tenants before uploading tenant information, photographs, documents, or related records to ZUZUStay.

We use mobile number verification through One-Time Passwords (OTP) to authenticate users and secure access to accounts.

2.3 Usage Information

We may automatically collect:

  • Diagnostic information

Users may request deletion of their account and associated data by sending an email to admin@zuzustay.com from their registered contact details or by providing sufficient account verification information.

Upon receiving a valid deletion request, we will process account and data deletion within 30 days, unless retention is required by applicable laws, regulatory requirements, dispute resolution obligations, or legitimate business purposes.

Once deletion is completed, access to the account and associated data may no longer be available.

9. Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations.

After account deletion, personal information will generally be removed within 30 days, except where retention is required by applicable laws, regulatory obligations, dispute resolution requirements, or legitimate business purposes.

ZUZUStay is intended for use by property owners, managers, operators, and authorized staff for property and tenant management purposes.

Users under the age of 18 should use the application only under appropriate supervision and authorization from a parent, guardian, property owner, or authorized manager.

We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under the age of 13. If we become aware that such information has been collected without appropriate authorization, we will take reasonable steps to delete it.
